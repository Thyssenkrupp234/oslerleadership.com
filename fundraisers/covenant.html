Welcome, to the Covenant House fundraiser. Here you can donate money to Covenant House through our Craft Sale.



Here is some information about Covenant House:
Covenant House helps homeless youth ages 16-24 who have fled emotional, physical, and sexual abuse.
It provides nutritious food, medical attention, and a secure sanctuary space for young at-risk people as well as supplying clothing and hygiene supplies.
Covenant House also offers community outreach services, a crisis program, mental health, and addiction support, and an inclusive space for LGBTQ2S+ youth.
