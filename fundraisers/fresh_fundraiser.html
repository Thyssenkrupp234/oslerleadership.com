Fresh to You Fundraiser (led by the Leadership team): bundles of locally grown fresh fruits and vegetables will be sold to raise money towards the Greater Vancouver Food Bank. The school receives 40% profit on all sales. In addition, each bundle sold will be matched to supply a complimentary bundle to families who have found it challenging to get fresh produce during Covid-19.

The fresh to you fundraiser has officially ended. Thank you for your support and donations.

For sale, there will be a choice between 2 bundles:
  • Bundle 1 ($20): 5 lb Pacific Sunset Potatoes, 3 lb Carrots, 3-pack Lovable Little Squash, 3 lb Yellow Onions
  • Bundle 2 ($25): 5 lb Pacific Sunrise Potatoes, 2 lb Carrots, 5 lb Red Beets, 5 lb Gala Apples, 0.5 lb Shallots
  • Fundraiser period: October 13 - October 27
  • Delivery: mid to late November
